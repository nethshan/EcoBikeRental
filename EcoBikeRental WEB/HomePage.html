Frequently Asked Questions

‚ûï How do I extend my bike rental?

You can extend your rental through the app by going to "My Rentals", selecting the active rental, and clicking "Extend". Extensions are subject to availability.

‚ûï What if the bike gets damaged?

Minor wear and tear is covered, but significant damage may incur additional charges. We recommend taking photos of the bike before your rental begins.

‚ûï How do I cancel my membership?

You can cancel your membership at any time through the Membership section. Cancellations take effect at the end of your current billing period.

‚ûï What happens if I return the bike late?

Late returns will be charged at 1.5x the hourly rate for each additional hour. If you know you'll be late, please extend your rental in advance.

‚ûï Can I reserve a specific bike?

Silver and Gold members can reserve specific bikes up to their allowed reservation window. Basic members can only select bike types, not specific bikes.
